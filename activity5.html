mango graham recipe

Ingredients

  • mango
  • graham
  • Condense milk
  • All purpose cream

Description

mango graham
A Filipino dessert that everyone love in summer,christmas birthday etc

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Chill all-purpose cream and condensed milk overnight. Peel and thinly slice 3-4 ripe mangoes (such as Ataulfo or Carabao)
  2. Make Cream Mixture: Whip the chilled all-purpose cream until stiff peaks form, then gently fold in the condensed milk until smooth and fluffy.
  3. Layer Dessert: Line the bottom of an 8x8 inch dish with a single layer of graham crackers. Spread a generous layer of the cream mixture over the crackers. Arrange a layer of thinly sliced mangoes evenly over the cream. Repeat the layers (crackers, cream, mango) 2-3 times, ending with a final layer of cream and mangoes.
  4. Chill and Serve: Cover and refrigerate for at least 3-4 hours, or overnight, to allow the graham crackers to soften and flavors to meld. Garnish with crushed graham crackers before serving cold.
